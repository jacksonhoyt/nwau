how to sign up

For leagues and tournaments, use the provided link to fill out a form, which usually takes 100 seconds to complete. We will ask for some of your most guarded secrets, like your name, experience and shirt size...
Leagues and tournaments will have a price listed. If you play in a league, you will need to buy a $25 NWAU membership, once per year.

For pick up games, no cost or sign up is required. Just show up, but letting us know you're coming would be smart. If pickup is cancelled for any reason, we will notify everyone on our pickup distro.

about us

This may be brief, but the best way to get to know us is by throwing a disc around.

Basically, we all wanted an organization that provides outlets to play the sport of Ultimate, so we made one. For some of us our college years are not far behind and some of us have teenagers, but our commonality lies in a love for the game and our community. We'd be thrilled to have you join us for pick up, leagues, tourneys, or happy hour.
Did you know Ultimate is an Olympic sport, now? Kids can learn it, daddy's with the dad gut can dominate the field like the old days, and every so often a true olympian emerges from the ranks. If you're inexperienced, a serious athlete, or a even a little out of shape this game is for you.

If you currently play in the area, why play in small divided groups across the area when we could play together and pool efforts to make consistent games happen?

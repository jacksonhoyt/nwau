about us

This may be brief, but the best way to get to know us is by throwing a disc around.

Basically, we all wanted an organization that provides outlets to play the sport of Ultimate, so we made one. Some of us are college aged and some of us have teenagers, but our commonality lies in a love for the game and our community. We'd be thrilled to have you join us for pick up, leagues, tourneys, or happy hour.
Did you know Ultimate is an Olympic sport, now? Kids can learn it, daddy's with the dad gut can keep up, and now olympians emerge. If you're inexperienced, out of shape, or a serious athlete this game is for you.

If you currently play in the area, why play in small divided groups across the area when we could play together and pool efforts to make consistent games happen?

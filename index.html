  • Find upcoming tournaments under Events.
  • Sign up and fill out all the required information.

  • Find times and places for pickup under Events.

about us

We are an organization committed to providing outlets and opportunities for the community to get out, get active, and get to know each other through the sport of Ultimate.

All ages and skill levels are welcome. Whether you are a long time veteran or you have never heard of the sport, we invite all to come out and participate in any of our events. We also want to teach the sport. Signing up for tournaments and leagues is a great way to start learning or be a teacher for others, depending where you find yourself. You can always learn as you go in one of our pick up games. Ultimate is a sport for all. No matter what your age, height, skill level, or what kind of shape you are in, there is a place for you on the Ultimate field!

Also, the sport of Ultimate is getting more recognition around the world. The sport has a professional league, world tournaments, and is officially eligible to be an Olympic game.

We look forward to spreading the great game of Ultimate throughout the community and are available to answer any and all questions. Hope to see you on the field!
